# Meal Plan Calculator

**A mobile-first meal planner for Indian food that turns calories, protein, diet, and taste preferences into an editable day of meals.**

[Open the planner](https://amitse.github.io/meal-plan-calculator/)

Plan a practical day of food without spreadsheets, rigid diet charts, or vague portions. Set your targets, generate a plan, then swap, delete, add, lock, and edit every item in **gm**.

## Why it helps

- **Built for familiar meals**: roti, rice, oats, poha, dosa, paneer, tofu, eggs, chicken/fish, and fruit snacks.
- **Made for your phone**: install it like an app, use it full-screen, and generate with a thumb-friendly button.
- **Actually customizable**: select multiple grains/proteins, avoid foods, swap items, delete items, add foods, and lock favorites.
- **Clear quantities**: meal items are shown in grams, with calories and protein visible at a glance.
- **Easy to share**: copy a share URL or export to CSV, Excel, or Google Docs.

## How to use it

1. Open the planner.
2. Enter calories, protein, and diet.
3. Customize food likes or avoids if needed.
4. Generate a plan.
5. Fine-tune each meal in gm: swap, delete, add, or lock items.
6. Share the link or export the plan.

## Best for

People planning Indian meals, vegetarians, eggetarians, non-veg eaters, macro trackers, and anyone who wants an editable meal plan instead of a one-size-fits-all diet chart.
